About Vertex

Built by people who'd rather ship than talk about shipping

We started Vertex because too many good ideas were dying in handoff meetings between design, dev and "the business." Nine years later, that's still the gap we close.

Our story

From a two-person favour to a 30-person studio

Vertex Digital Solutions began in 2017 when two engineers fixed a friend's failing checkout flow over a weekend. The fix held. So did the next one, and the one after that.

Today we're a 30-person studio split across engineering, design and cloud operations, but the original instinct hasn't changed: understand the actual problem before opening an editor, and don't consider a project finished until it survives contact with real users.

2017

Vertex is founded

Two engineers, one client, a single shared laptop charger.

2019

First enterprise client

Took on our first long-term retainer, building out a healthcare scheduling platform.

2022

Cloud & DevOps practice launches

Grew from "we also do hosting" into a dedicated infrastructure team.

2026

120+ products shipped

Now a 30-person team working with clients across 14 countries.

What we believe

The principles behind every engagement

01

Default to clarity

A roadmap nobody believes in is worse than no roadmap. We'd rather say "we don't know yet" than pad an estimate.

02

Ship in small, real increments

Working software in front of real users beats a polished demo every time. We aim for the former from week one.

03

Own the outcome, not just the ticket

If a feature technically works but doesn't solve the problem, it isn't done. We measure ourselves on the second part.

04

Leave things better than we found them

That includes your codebase, your team's confidence, and the next developer who has to read our work.
