What we offer

Four disciplines. One accountable team.

Whether you need a single feature built or a full platform rebuilt, we scope it the same way: understand the problem, agree the smallest useful version, then build.

Web Development

Custom web applications, customer portals and marketing sites built on modern, maintainable stacks — React, Node.js, and well-documented APIs. We design for the load you'll actually have, not a hypothetical peak.

Mobile App Development

iOS and Android apps from a shared codebase where it makes sense, native where it doesn't. We handle app store submission, push notifications and offline support.

Cloud & DevOps

Infrastructure-as-code, CI/CD pipelines, and monitoring set up so deploys are routine, not stressful. We work across AWS, GCP and Azure depending on what you already use.

IT Strategy & Consulting

Technical audits, vendor evaluations and roadmaps for teams deciding what to build, buy, or retire. Useful before you commit a budget, not just after something breaks.

UI/UX Design

Research-backed product design — wireframes, prototypes and design systems your engineering team can actually implement without guesswork.

Data & Analytics

Dashboards, pipelines and reporting that turn scattered logs and spreadsheets into decisions your team can actually act on.

Common questions

Before you reach out

How long does a typical project take?

Most scoped projects run 6–12 weeks. We'll give you a real estimate after the discovery call, once we understand what you actually need.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes — about a third of our clients are pre-Series A. We scope tightly so you're not paying for more than the current stage needs.

Can you take over an existing codebase?

Often, yes. We start with a short paid audit so we (and you) know what we're inheriting before committing to a timeline.

What does the support window after launch include?

Bug fixes, monitoring, and minor adjustments for 30 days after go-live. Larger feature work is scoped separately.

Not sure which plan fits?

Tell us about the project — we'll point you to the right one.
